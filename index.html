Voight-Kampff
Intro

The Voight-Kampff is an empathy test given to determine whether a subject is human or android.

Directions:

You will be given a series of questions, you are to do the following as quickly as possible:

  1. Determine the subject to be empathetic towards
  2. Determine the type of empathy you feel
  3. Press the key corresponding to the type of empathy you feel ("A") for acceptable, ("D") for disgusting, and ("J") for just neutral

Responses are graded on how accurate the type of empathy is, as well as the response time
